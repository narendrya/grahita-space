About Us

GrahitaNet is built upon the words "grahita" and "net". The word "grahita" came from the Sanskrit word which means to understand — as that is our vision to help better understand everyone, primarily students. The word "net" in our case has 2 different meanings: firstly it is simply to say that we are a network; the second meaning means to capture the mental problems before it causes any more damage to the students' wellbeing.

⚠️ Peringatan: Beban Belajar Terdeteksi Overload!

Sistem Grahita mendeteksi lonjakan stres. Direkomendasikan melakukan langkah intervensi berikut:

  • Teknik Pernapasan 4-7-8: Tarik napas melalui hidung 4 detik, tahan 7 detik, embuskan lewat mulut perlahan 8 detik.
  • Reminder Istirahat: Jauhkan pandangan dari tugas selama 5 menit untuk menyegarkan saraf kognitif. Disarankan tidur lebih awal dan kurangi screen time.
  • Peregangan Motorik: Regangkan otot bahu dan leher untuk mengurangi tegangan fisik.
